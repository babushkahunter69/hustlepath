## Use tools that reduce friction

Tools should make your work easier, not become another thing to obsess over.

Start with free tools until you know what you actually need.

## A simple beginner toolkit

You can start with:

- Canva for graphics
- Google Docs for drafts
- Notion or Trello for planning
- Pinterest for traffic
- GitHub and Vercel for simple publishing

## Avoid buying too early

Paid tools are useful later. In the beginning, your real job is to publish, test, and learn what gets attention.

## Final takeaway

Pick a small tool stack and stick to it long enough to build momentum.
